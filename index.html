How to Start Trading

Use the Deposit form (upper left) to put your funds (ETH and tokens) on the token market. Do not send your tokens directly to the smart contract, or they will be lost and unrecoverable. Creedexchange cannot help you with funds incorrectly deposited to the trading smart contract.
